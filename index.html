NotQuiteTher

an e-reader/game in development

NotQuiteTher runs on Project Gutenberg books. Project Gutenberg is a library of over 60k free e-books. Founded in 1971. Built by volunteers. Formats for Kindle, EPUB, HTML & Plain text. Focused on books with expired U.S. copyrights.

NotQuiteTher picks books at random, based on the criteria (if any) selected by the user. Choose to see books within a given level of popularity. Or see more recent uploads instead. The default criteria is for books with 5k+ downloads (as of early 2022)
